AviationStack Access Key
Powers the live ATL departures board — real gate numbers, delays & status. Free plan: 100 req/month.
How to get a free key
1Go to aviationstack.com/signup/free and create a free account
2Copy your access_key from the dashboard
3Paste it above and click Save & Connect
4Free plan = HTTP endpoint — a CORS proxy is applied automatically. Upgrade to $49.99+/mo for direct HTTPS.
⚠️ Free plan uses HTTP. A public CORS proxy (corsproxy.io) is used automatically — fine for demos, upgrade for production.

How to get a free Apify token
1Sign up at apify.com — free $5 monthly credit included
2Go to Settings → Integrations and copy your Personal API token
3Paste it here — prices load asynchronously when you select a flight (~30 sec)
ℹ️ Prices load asynchronously via Google Flights scraper. No extra setup needed beyond the token.
